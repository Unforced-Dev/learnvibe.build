// design

Design is a conversation
between you and the future.

A wireframe, a PRD, a napkin sketch — they're all the same thing: communicating intent. To yourself, to the AI, to anyone who works on this next.

// design

Layers of fidelity

→ A bullet list — is design

→ A conversation — is design

→ A PRD — is design

→ A mockup — is design

Match the fidelity to the stakes. You don't always need a Figma file.

D3

Development

You are the taste. The AI is the hands.

// development

Reading code you didn't write

→ You don't need to understand every line

→ You need to understand the shape

→ Does this look like what I asked for?

→ Are there files I didn't expect?

The AI is tireless but tasteless. Your job is the review.

// development

The review cycle

Build → Look → Adjust → Build

→ CLAUDE.md — teach the AI about your project. Like onboarding a new team member.

→ Compound knowledge — every project teaches you patterns. It gets easier.

// deployment

Shipping changes
who you are.

Before this cohort, most of you wouldn't have called yourselves builders. Now you've built things. That's not a certificate — it's a lived experience.

// deployment

"Done" beats "perfect"

→ Perfectionism is procrastination in a costume

→ Ship the version that works. Improve later.

→ Build in public. Share the URL. Put it in your bio. Tell someone.

The act of sharing completes the creative cycle.
