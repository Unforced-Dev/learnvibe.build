// what_you_learn

From idea to reality

01

AI as Thinking Partner

Use Claude to organize thoughts, explore ideas, and turn messy input into structured output. The fundamentals that make everything else possible.

02

Building with Claude Code

Go from conversation to code. Build websites, tools, and applications through dialogue. No prior coding experience required.

03

Shipping Real Things

Deploy your projects to the world. Git, hosting, domains — the practical skills to get your work live and shareable.

04

Learning Together

Cohort-based for a reason. You learn from each other's projects, questions, and discoveries. Community that continues after the program ends.
