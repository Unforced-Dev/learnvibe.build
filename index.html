01 — The Idea

Articulation is the new skill

You don't need four years of engineering school. You need an idea and the words to bring it to life. AI changes what's possible — if you know how to work with it.

Learn Vibe Build teaches you to use Claude as a thinking partner and building collaborator. Not prompt engineering tricks — real creative partnership with AI. The skill being developed is clarity: learning to see what you want to make and how to move toward it.

Play Before Polish

Start by exploring, not by planning. The best projects emerge from the act of making. We build in the margins, ship early, and let the work teach us what it needs to become.

Ship Real Things

Not exercises. Not sandboxes. Real projects, deployed to the internet, that do something meaningful. You leave with a URL, not just a certificate.

03 — What You Build
