// what_it_is

Learn together. Build together.

This isn't a course where you watch videos alone. It's a cohort — 25 people learning from each other, building alongside each other, and shipping together.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Cohort-based

You're not just learning from instructors — you're learning from each other. 25 motivated people, all building at the same time.

🛠️ Create something real

You'll leave with something you made — a website, a newsletter system, a tool, whatever serves your vision. Demo day to share it.

📅 4 weeks

Weekly live sessions + office hours. Enough structure to keep momentum, enough space to find your own flow.

🎤 Demo Day

Culminates Feb 13 with a demo day to showcase what you created. Share your work with the community.

// your_guide

Who you'll learn with

Aaron Gabriel

Founder of Parachute

I've spent the last year building with AI every day — first as an experiment, then as my primary way of working. I built Parachute, a voice-first tool for connected thinking, almost entirely through conversation with Claude. Now I use Parachute as my bridge to Claude Code to develop Parachute itself and all my other projects.

I'm a graduate student at CU Boulder's ATLAS Creative Technology program, founder of Woven Web, and I build technology for others through Unforced Development. But mostly I'm someone who believes that the best software comes from understanding people first, code second.

This cohort is me sharing what I've learned about building with AI, and facilitating a space where we can all learn and grow together.
