Vibe.coding

From idea to software with AI as your creative partner.

Learn Vibe Build · Spring 2026

The bottleneck isn't coding.
It's clarity.

AI can write code faster than any of us. The hard part is knowing what you want — and being able to communicate it clearly.

Vibecoding is the practice of turning your ideas into working software through conversation with AI.

Five steps.

  • Communicate Context — Get your thinking into the computer
  • Design Iteratively — Shape ideas into form with quick experiments
  • Make a Plan — Create a shared understanding with your agent
  • Let 'er Rip — Execute, then verify
  • Compound — Let every session make the next one better
1

Communicate context

Getting your thinking into the computer is lesson number one. The idea in your head needs to become words the AI can work with.

Typing helps you slow down and look at the shape of your thinking. Voice lets you get context out of your brain and onto the computer quickly — messy thoughts and all.

Handy (free) SuperWhisper Wispr Flow Monologue
2

Design & think iteratively

Use AI to start shaping your ideas into form. Don't try to build the whole thing — start with small prototypes that help you refine your own thinking.

  • Quick documents to articulate your vision
  • Simple websites or landing pages as props for thinking
  • Designs and mockups with tools like Pencil.dev
  • Each experiment teaches you what you actually want
3

Make a plan

Once you know what you're building, the first step is to generate a detailed plan with your agent.

  • Be specific — vague plans create vague code
  • Ensure you and your agent have a shared understanding
  • Include strategies for testing
  • A good plan means the agent can work autonomously for longer
4

Let 'er rip

A good plan means the agent can work for a long time on its own, implementing what you designed together.

  • Always manually test what it builds
  • Have the agent review its own code before submitting
  • You are the taste — the agent is the hands
Plan → Build → Review → Test → Ship
5

Compound your learnings

Figure out what works and let those learnings compound — not just in how you prompt, but in the system itself.

  • Refine your prompting instincts with each session
  • Put learnings into CLAUDE.md or AGENTS.md
  • Your agent orients itself better automatically next time
  • Every project makes the next one easier
With great power comes great responsibility.

Review what it builds. Understand what it does. Stay grounded.
